Welcome to Prefectural!

Based on the trend of Wordle-inspired games, Prefectural gives you a random Japanese prefecture each day for you to guess. The prefecture is determined using the date, so on a given day it will be the same for everyone.

You have 6 tries to guess the prefecture highlighted on the map in yellow. Type the name of the prefecture in the box, or select it from the dropdown. If the highlighted prefecture is hard to make out, you can hover your mouse over the map to zoom in.
